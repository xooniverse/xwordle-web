Wordle Bot Notice

Dear Wordle Enthusiasts,

It is with a heavy heart that we announce the temporary discontinuation of the Wordle Bot. Due to budget constraints, we at Xooniverse are unable to sustain the operation of the bot. Thank you for your understanding and continued support.
